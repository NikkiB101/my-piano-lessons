Adult Piano Age 18 and Up

Adult Piano

Adult Piano lessons are suitable for adults of all ages. Whether you have absolutely no experience or are a seasoned player we can help you advance your playing ability.

Your lessons are individually tailored to you. You will be introduced to new concepts through learning your favourite songs which keep you motivated to learn. Our teachers will go at your pace no matter how fast or slow this is.

Our main aim is that you enjoy each lesson and make progress on your piano learning journey. Each of our adult teachers have been chosen specifically for their ability to pass on their knowledge in an easy to understand, fun manner.

Examinations

Examinations can be taken by adults of all ages. For those who want to follow a defined syllabus examinations can provide a more structured learning environment however it can be a little less flexible.

Many of our adult students choose to learn piano purely for their own enjoyment and do not always participate in examinations.

Our teachers are happy to prepare you for any examinations you wish. Please let your teacher know whether or not you wish to take exams.

How Online Lessons Work?

Getting started with online lessons is really straight forward.

You simply need to Set Up A Zoom Account. Once your account is created you then let your teacher know the email address you used when setting up your account.

Your child's teacher will call you on Zoom at their lesson time. You can use a mobile, tablet, laptop or computer to take the call. If you have more than one of these options we suggest using the one with the biggest screen.

Online lessons save you time and money with no need to travel. They are also a great way to keep your child engaged in extra curricular activities during the pandemic.

For further information check out Video Tutorials on Zoom..

Book Now

Adult Piano Lessons are available Monday to Thursday from 2.00pm to 6.30pm. When booking you will be asked to select any day/time which does not fit your schedule.

A member of our team will then contact you with a list of times available and get you booked in. If none of these times suit you we will then place you on our waiting list until we have a time available which suits you.

Please note lessons cannot be cancelled during term. If you will not be able to attend your lesson please let us know at the start of term and we will adjust your fees accordingly.

Missed lessons cannot be transferred to another student or rescheduled unless agreed by the school in writing at the start of term.
