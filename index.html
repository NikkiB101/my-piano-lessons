Welcome to My Piano Lessons

If you are thinking of getting piano lessons then you have come to the right place. My Piano Lessons has a range of lessons suited to students of all ages. Whether you are a complete beginner or an experienced player who wants to return to lessons we have a number of options suited to you. Each of our lessons are individually tailored to your wants and needs. So you can choose to play your favourite songs, take examinations or both. Its your music, your way! Take a look at the piano lessons we offer below.

Junior Piano

Our Junior Music Academy is suited to children from age 3 to 12. Lessons are designed to be fun and interactive so any child attending our lessons will develop a love of music. We encourage this love of music in each lessons by teaching children their favourite songs. Children can also start examination at a young age if they wish.

Adult Piano

If you are an adult hoping to start or return to piano lessons our friendly teachers will happily chat to you about what you want to cover. We are happy to move at a faster or slower pace depending on your needs. We know that some adult students can be nervous starting a new activity but our teachers will ensure you have a really positive experience with us.

Teen Piano

For students between the ages of 12 to 18 we offer our Teen Music Academy. In teen music lessons students are often prepared for Junior and Leaving Certificate practical examinations. In addition to this we continue to encourage a love of music by allowing students to learn music they wish to learn no matter what genre that may be.
