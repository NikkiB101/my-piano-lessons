Junior Music

Junior Music is suited to children between the ages of 4 and 11. Our lessons will follow your child's own learning pace to ensure they always find lessons rewarding.

We will ask for a list of your child's favourite songs before they start lessons with us. We then use this list to introduce new concepts. Your child is then motivated to learn each new concept as once they understand the new concept, they are rewarded by being able to play their favourite song.

We know that some children like Jazz and others like Rock n Roll, we understand when it comes to Junior Music one size does not fit all. Our teachers are extremely flexible and are happy to discuss your child's learning with you at any stage during their lessons.

Take a look below to find out more about online lessons, examinations, schedules, fees and more.

Examinations

For many children completing examinations can be a great way to mark the progress they have made identify the standard of playing they have achieved. Formal recognition of their abilities is very beneficial especially if they hope to pursue music in secondary school or college.

We offer examinations with all exam boards and your child's teacher will discuss the differentoptions open to your child when it comes time for exam preparation. The teacher will review the exam syllabi and suggest which exam board would be most suited to your child however this is only a suggestion. If you want your child to take a particular exam please let your child's teacher know.

Examinations whilst encouraged are not compulsory. If you would prefer that your child does not complete examinations simply let your child's teacher know.

How Online Lessons Work?

Getting started with online lessons is really straight forward.

You simply need to Set Up A Zoom Account. Once your account is created you then let your teacher know the email address you used when setting up your account.

Your child's teacher will call you on Zoom at their lesson time. You can use a mobile, tablet, laptop or computer to take the call. If you have more than one of these options we suggest using the one with the biggest screen.

Online lessons save you time and money with no need to travel. They are also a great way to keep your child engaged in extra curricular activities during the pandemic.
