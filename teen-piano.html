Teen Piano Age 12 to 18

Teen Piano

Teen Piano lessons are designed for 12 to 18 year olds. Whether you are a beginner or a more experienced player we can help you improve your piano skills quickly. Some teenage beginners can progress rapidly if they are willing to put in a little extra progress.

We do not have a fixed syllabi. Each lesson is tailor made for the student so they can progress as quickly as they want. We regularly have students skip exams if their progress is moving so rapidly that examination dates can't keep up.

In our piano lessons, we teach key concepts by allowing the student to learn their favourite songs. This keeps our students motivated and builds their confidence in their playing ability.

Examinations

For many teenagers, examinations are an important part of progression. Many teenage students will be taking music at second level and will be attending lessons to prepare for their practical exam. As the practical exam accounts so many marks, lessons can be an excellent way for teenagers to get a competitive advantage in the CAO system.

We offer examinations with all exam boards and your piano teacher will discuss the different options with you during lessons when it comes time for exam preparation. I tis not unusual for our teachers to prepare you for your practical examination a few levels higher than you are currently at.

Teachers may indicate that a particular piece at a higher level would suit your playing ability and may be an opportunity for them to receive higher marks. Each teacher understands the importance of preparation for both music exams and state exams and will be completely flexible to meet your requirements.

How Online Lessons Work?

Getting started with online lessons is really straight forward.

You simply need to Set Up A Zoom Account. Once your account is created you then let your teacher know the email address you used when setting up your account.

Your child's teacher will call you on Zoom at their lesson time. You can use a mobile, tablet, laptop or computer to take the call. If you have more than one of these options we suggest using the one with the biggest screen.

Online lessons save you time and money with no need to travel. They are also a great way to keep your child engaged in extra curricular activities during the pandemic.

Book Now

Teen Music Lessons are available Monday to Thursday from 2.00pm to 6.30pm. When booking you will be asked to select any day/time which does not fit your schedule.

A member of our team will then contact you with a list of times available and get you booked in. If none of these times suit you we will then place you on our waiting list until we have a time available which suits you.

Please note lessons cannot be cancelled during term. If you will not be able to attend your lesson please let us know at the start of term and we will adjust your fees accordingly.

Missed lessons cannot be transferred to another student or rescheduled unless agreed by the school in writing at the start of term.
